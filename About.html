My name is James Kalunga, i am originaly from central Africa, D.R.Congo.My familly and i were resetled as refugees in Tucson Arizona on the 14th of December 2007, where i had a chance to attend high school for two years and graduated. After high school i was granted a scholarship at GCU in Phoenix where i did my undergad in International Relations. After, i decided to go and give back to my fellow refugees by working with a refugee resettlment agency.I worked for aproximately five years.As years went by i started to feel bored of living in AZ, especially summer was extremely hot for me. My wife and i decided to relocate to Georgia in october 2018 and now here i am.
